Blast Alley

Two-player matches run over your Wi-Fi network, phone to phone, no server. Both phones need to be on the same network.

How to play

Drop bombs to blow up soft crates and trap your rivals. Blasts travel in a cross and stop at steel walls. Anything caught in the flame dies, including you.

Fire lengthens your blast. Bomb lets you drop one more at a time. Boot makes you faster.

Keyboard: arrows or WASD to move, space to drop. Touch: D-pad and the round button. Last one standing wins. Three minutes, then it's a draw.
